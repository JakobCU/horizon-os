# Partner Outreach Email Template

> **Used by**: Consortium Scout (drafts), User (sends)
> **Customize**: Replace all `[BRACKETED]` placeholders

---

## Template

**Subject**: Invitation to join Horizon Europe consortium — [CALL_ID] — [PROPOSAL_ACRONYM]

Dear [TITLE] [LAST_NAME],

I am writing to invite [ORGANIZATION_NAME] to join a consortium we are assembling for the upcoming Horizon Europe call **[CALL_ID]: [TOPIC_TITLE]**, with a submission deadline of **[DEADLINE_DATE]**.

**About the proposal**

Our project, **[PROPOSAL_ACRONYM]** ("[PROPOSAL_FULL_TITLE]"), aims to [2-3 SENTENCE SUMMARY OF THE PROPOSAL CONCEPT — sourced from proposal_concept_live.md]. The consortium is coordinated by [COORDINATOR_NAME] ([COORDINATOR_COUNTRY]) and currently comprises [N] partners from [N] countries.

**Why [ORGANIZATION_NAME]?**

We are particularly interested in your organization because of [SPECIFIC EVIDENCE OF FIT — reference their past EU projects, publications, domain expertise, infrastructure, or unique capabilities]. Your expertise in [SPECIFIC_CAPABILITY] would be invaluable for [WP_NUMBER]: [WP_TITLE], where we envision your team contributing to [SPECIFIC_TASKS — e.g., "developing the multilingual NLP pipeline for real-time threat detection across EU languages"].

**Envisioned role**

- **Work Packages**: [WP_NUMBERS]
- **Tasks**: [BRIEF TASK DESCRIPTION]
- **Estimated effort**: [X] person-months over [DURATION] months
- **Estimated budget**: approximately EUR [AMOUNT] (subject to consortium discussion)
- **Role**: [WP Lead / Task Lead / Partner]

**Next steps**

If you are interested, we would appreciate receiving:
1. A brief expression of interest (email reply is sufficient)
2. A short capability statement (1 page) highlighting relevant expertise and infrastructure
3. CV(s) of the key person(s) who would be involved

We kindly ask for your response by **[RESPONSE_DEADLINE — typically 2-3 weeks before internal consortium deadline]**, so we can finalize the consortium composition and begin drafting.

I would be happy to schedule a call to discuss the proposal in more detail. Please let me know your availability.

Thank you for considering this opportunity. We believe that [ORGANIZATION_NAME]'s participation would significantly strengthen the consortium and the proposal.

Best regards,

[SENDER_NAME]
[SENDER_TITLE]
[SENDER_ORGANIZATION]
[SENDER_EMAIL]
[SENDER_PHONE]
