## Destination Overview

Cross-cutting security research and innovation topics, including:
- Security research governance and coordination
- Standardization and certification for security technologies
- Pre-commercial procurement of innovative security solutions
- Innovation uptake by security practitioners
- Foresight and horizon scanning for emerging security threats

## Key Considerations for Proposals

- **Innovation uptake focus** — bridging the gap between research results and operational use
- **Standardization** — proposals should plan concrete contributions to standards bodies
- **Practitioner engagement** — direct involvement of security practitioners in defining requirements and validating solutions
- **Coordination with other destinations** — SSRI topics often complement specific topics in Destinations 1-3

## Relevant Activities

- European Security Research and Innovation Forum (ESRIF legacy)
- Community of Users on Secure, Safe and Resilient Societies (CoU)
- ENLETS (European Network of Law Enforcement Technology Services)
- Pre-commercial procurement initiatives
