> **Cluster 3, Destination 3**: Disaster-Resilient Society
> **Note**: Populate with current work programme details for the relevant call year

## Destination Overview

Research and innovation for a disaster-resilient society, covering:
- All phases of disaster management (prevention, preparedness, response, recovery)
- Natural and man-made disasters
- Multi-hazard and cascading effects
- Climate change adaptation in disaster management
- Critical infrastructure resilience

## Key Considerations for Proposals

- **Multi-hazard approach** — proposals should not be single-hazard unless the call specifies
- **Practitioner involvement** — civil protection authorities, first responders, crisis managers
- **CER Directive alignment** — for critical infrastructure topics (see `knowledge/eu_policies/cer_directive.md`)
- **Climate-disaster nexus** — link to Green Deal and climate adaptation
- **UNDRR Sendai Framework** — reference the global framework for disaster risk reduction
- **Cross-border** — EU added value through cross-border disaster response

## Relevant EU Strategies and Frameworks

- EU Civil Protection Mechanism (UCPM)
- CER Directive (2022/2557)
- EU Adaptation Strategy
- Sendai Framework for Disaster Risk Reduction 2015-2030
- European Climate Pact
