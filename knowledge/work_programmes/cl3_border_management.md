# CL3 Work Programme — Border Management

> **Cluster 3, Destination 2**: Border Management
> **Note**: Populate with current work programme details for the relevant call year

## Destination Overview

Research and innovation for effective and efficient integrated border management, including:
- Detection, identification, and tracking at borders
- Maritime and land border surveillance
- Document and identity verification
- Customs and supply chain security
- Search and rescue at sea borders

## Key Considerations for Proposals

- **Frontex (EBCGA) involvement** — highly valued; proposals should engage with the Frontex research framework
- **eu-LISA** — for IT systems interoperability (EES, ETIAS, SIS, VIS, Eurodac)
- **Fundamental rights at borders** — critical; proposals must address rights of travelers, migrants, asylum seekers
- **Interoperability** — with existing EU IT systems and national border systems
- **Dual-use / ethical scrutiny** — border surveillance technologies face heightened ethical review

## Relevant EU Strategies and Systems

- EU Integrated Border Management Strategy
- Smart Borders package (EES, ETIAS)
- Schengen Information System (SIS)
- Interoperability framework (EU 2019/817 and 2019/818)
- Frontex Capability Roadmap
