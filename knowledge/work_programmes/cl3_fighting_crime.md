# CL3 Work Programme — Fighting Crime and Terrorism

> **Cluster 3, Destination 1**: Fighting Crime and Terrorism
> **Note**: Populate with current work programme details for the relevant call year

## Destination Overview

Research and innovation to support the fight against crime and terrorism, addressing:
- Prevention, investigation, and mitigation of criminal and terrorist activities
- Understanding crime and terrorism (including cybercrime, organized crime, terrorism)
- Forensics and digital evidence
- Border management aspects related to crime prevention

## Typical Topics

- Advanced tools for financial investigations and asset recovery
- AI-based tools for law enforcement (with fundamental rights safeguards)
- Digital forensics and electronic evidence
- Prevention of radicalization
- Counter-terrorism technologies and approaches
- Cybercrime investigation tools
- Combating trafficking in human beings

## Key Considerations for Proposals

- **LEA involvement is typically mandatory** — real end-users, not consultants
- **Ethics and fundamental rights** — heightened scrutiny for crime/terrorism topics
- **Dual-use risk** — surveillance and investigation tools require careful misuse assessment
- **Data sensitivity** — often involves law enforcement data, requiring LED compliance
- **Practitioner-driven** — proposals should start from practitioner needs, not technology push
