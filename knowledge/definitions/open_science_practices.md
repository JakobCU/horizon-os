# Open Science Practices — Horizon Europe Requirements

> **Source**: Horizon Europe MGA Art. 17, Open Science provisions
> **Used by**: Excellence Architect (Section 1.3)

## Mandatory Open Science Requirements

### 1. Open Access to Publications
- **Immediate open access** to peer-reviewed publications (no embargo)
- Deposit in a trusted repository (e.g., Zenodo, institutional repository)
- Under a CC-BY license (or CC-BY-NC for justified cases)
- This is a **legal obligation** under the MGA, not optional

### 2. Research Data Management
- **Data Management Plan (DMP)** — deliverable due within first 6 months
- Apply **FAIR principles**: Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable
- Deposit data in trusted repositories with persistent identifiers (DOIs)
- "As open as possible, as closed as necessary" — justified exceptions for:
  - Security-classified data
  - Personal data (GDPR)
  - IP that will be commercially exploited
  - Data subject to third-party restrictions

### 3. Open Access to Research Data
- Default: open access to data needed to validate publications
- Exceptions must be justified (security, privacy, IP)
- For CL3 proposals: significant portions of data may be restricted — this is acceptable but must be explicitly justified

## Recommended (Not Mandatory) Practices

- Open-source software (especially for publicly funded tools)
- Pre-registration of research protocols
- Open peer review
- Citizen science engagement
- Use of open standards and interoperable formats

## Template Text for Proposals

> "The project is committed to open science principles as required by Horizon Europe. All peer-reviewed publications will be made immediately available in open access under CC-BY licenses, deposited in [repository]. A Data Management Plan will be delivered in M6 (D[X.Y]) following FAIR principles. Research data underlying publications will be made openly available via [repository], except where restrictions are necessary due to [security classification / personal data protection / commercial exploitation]. Software tools developed in WP[X] will be released under [license] on [platform]."
