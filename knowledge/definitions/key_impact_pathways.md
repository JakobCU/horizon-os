# Key Impact Pathways — Horizon Europe Reference

> **Source**: Horizon Europe Strategic Plan, Key Impact Pathways
> **Used by**: Impact Specialist

## Horizon Europe Key Impact Pathways (KIPs)

The EC defines impact through three dimensions:

### 1. Scientific Impact
- Creating high-quality new knowledge
- Strengthening human capital in R&I
- Fostering diffusion of knowledge and open science

**Indicators**: Publications, citations, open access rates, trained researchers, research infrastructures

### 2. Societal Impact
- Addressing EU policy priorities and global challenges
- Delivering benefits through R&I missions
- Strengthening uptake of R&I in society

**Indicators**: Policy uptake, standardization contributions, solutions to societal challenges, citizen engagement

### 3. Economic Impact
- Generating innovation-based growth
- Creating more and better jobs
- Leveraging investments in R&I

**Indicators**: IP generated, spin-offs created, SME growth, investment leveraged, standards established
