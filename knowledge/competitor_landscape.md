## Why This Matters

Most Horizon Europe CL3 calls fund 1-3 projects from 10-20 submissions. Knowing who else is likely applying — and what angle they'll take — is critical for differentiation and consortium strategy. If your main competitor has Europol, you need a different end-user anchor. If three proposals will push AI, maybe your angle is human factors.
