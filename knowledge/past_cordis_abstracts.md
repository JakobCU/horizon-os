# Past CORDIS Abstracts — Reference Database

## Theme: First Responder Wearables & Biosignal Monitoring

### SIXTHSENSE — 883315
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2019
- **Duration**: 2020 to 2023
- **Coordinator**: TECNALIA (Spain)
- **Key Focus**: Wearable health monitoring with biosensors and tactile biofeedback for first responders. Early detection of risk factors via multimodal biosensor data. Field-tested with 50+ firefighters.
- **TRL Reached**: 6 (field trials in relevant environment)
- **Relevance**: Closest precursor to CORTEX BB1 (biosignal platform). Did NOT integrate into C2 — the gap CORTEX fills.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/883315

### FASTER — 833507
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2018
- **Duration**: 2019 to 2022
- **Coordinator**: CERTH (Greece)
- **Key Focus**: Smart textiles with physiological sensors, IoT platform aggregating wearable data + social media + environment sensors into a Common Operational Picture. 13 demonstrations across 8 countries.
- **TRL Reached**: 6-7
- **Relevance**: Demonstrated sensor-to-COP pipeline but without cognitive state inference or adaptive C2.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/833507

### RESCUER — 101021836
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2020
- **Duration**: 2021 to 2024
- **Key Focus**: First responder toolkit with biosignal monitoring, cognitive load estimation fed into AR framework. Adaptive information display based on cognitive load state. Mountain rescue, earthquake, tunnel scenarios.
- **TRL Reached**: 5-6
- **Relevance**: Most direct precursor to CORTEX adaptive interface concept. Limited scope, ended at TRL 5-6. CORTEX extends this to full C2 integration at TRL 7-8.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101021836

## Theme: Human-AI Teaming, Drones/Robots for SAR

### INGENIOUS — 833435
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2018
- **Duration**: 2019 to 2023
- **Coordinator**: ICCS (Greece)
- **Key Focus**: Integrated toolkit: robotics + IoT + wearables + AR + drones. Novel C3 and Common Operational Picture platform. 12 products identified near market-ready.
- **TRL Reached**: 6-7
- **Relevance**: Comprehensive integration effort but human factors was secondary. Market uptake unclear.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/833435

### RESPOND-A — 883371
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2019
- **Duration**: 2020 to 2023
- **Key Focus**: 5G-enabled platform integrating AR/VR, autonomous robots/UAVs, wearable sensors, 360-degree video streaming, geovisual analytics.
- **TRL Reached**: 5-6
- **Relevance**: Demonstrated AR + robots + wearables integration; CORTEX builds on this with cognitive state-driven adaptation.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/883371

### RESPONDRONE — 833717
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2018
- **Duration**: 2019 to 2022
- **Coordinator**: DLR (Germany)
- **Key Focus**: Multi-drone fleet with synchronized missions for disaster response. Web-based C2 system. Tested in civil protection exercises on Corsica.
- **TRL Reached**: 6
- **Relevance**: Drone C2 integration — potential building block for CORTEX BB5.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/833717

### CURSOR — 832790
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2018
- **Duration**: 2019 to 2022
- **Key Focus**: Miniaturized robots (SMURFs) with chemical sensors + drone fleet for SAR under collapsed structures.
- **TRL Reached**: 5-6
- **Relevance**: Confined-space robotics — relevant to CORTEX BB5 scenarios.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/832790

### TeamAware — 101019808
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2020
- **Duration**: 2021 to 2024
- **Coordinator**: SOFTWARE IMAGINATION & VISION (Romania)
- **Key Focus**: Integrated situational awareness system: drone-mounted, wearable + external sensors. Standardized AR/mobile HMI.
- **TRL Reached**: 5-6
- **Relevance**: SA system integration; CORTEX extends with cognitive state-driven adaptation.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101019808

### TRIFFID — 101168042
- **Call**: Horizon Europe CL3
- **Duration**: 2024-ongoing
- **Coordinator**: Harokopio University (Greece)
- **Key Focus**: Hybrid UGV+UAV robotic platform with AI-driven autonomy. AR ground station interface with semantic mapping. Wildfires, floods, earthquake SAR.
- **TRL Target**: 6-7
- **Relevance**: ONGOING — represents current SotA in SAR robotics. Does NOT address biosignal monitoring or adaptive human-AI teaming. CORTEX is complementary.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101168042

### HURRICANE — 101168017
- **Call**: Horizon Europe CL3
- **Duration**: 2024-ongoing
- **Key Focus**: UGV in hazardous environments with enhanced vision, UAV-UGV cooperation, 5G comms. Training 100 FRs across 15 countries.
- **TRL Target**: 6-7
- **Relevance**: ONGOING — robot-focused SAR. No human factors / cognitive state component. Complementary to CORTEX.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101168017

### PROACTIF — 101194239
- **Call**: Horizon Europe CL3
- **Duration**: 2024-ongoing
- **Key Focus**: 42-partner consortium: multi-domain UxV teaming (UAV+USV+UGV) with mission control for SAR, wildfire, critical infrastructure.
- **TRL Target**: 6-7
- **Relevance**: ONGOING — massive robotics platform effort. CORTEX addresses the human layer that PROACTIF does not.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101194239

## Theme: XR Training for First Responders

### SHOTPROS — 833672
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2018
- **Duration**: 2019 to 2022
- **Coordinator**: [Austria-based]
- **Key Focus**: VR-based training for police decision-making under stress. Human factors research on stress/cognitive performance.
- **TRL Reached**: 6
- **Relevance**: **AIT CTE was a partner.** Direct pedigree for CORTEX XR training module. CORTEX extends to human-AI teaming scenarios.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/833672

### MED1stMR — 101021775
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2020
- **Duration**: 2021 to 2024
- **Key Focus**: Mixed reality training for medical first responders with haptic manikins. Mass casualty, tunnel collapse, terrorist attack scenarios.
- **TRL Reached**: 6
- **Relevance**: **AIT CTE was a partner.** Green Manikin MR training demonstrator. CORTEX builds on this for SAR-specific human-AI teaming training.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101021775

### ASSISTANCE — 832576
- **Call**: H2020-SU-SEC-2018
- **Duration**: 2019 to 2023
- **Key Focus**: VR/MR/AR training for first responders + operational platform with drones, robots, wearable sensors.
- **TRL Reached**: 5-6
- **Relevance**: Combined training + operations; CORTEX focuses more deeply on the human-AI teaming dimension.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/832576

## Theme: Cognitive-Adaptive Interfaces (Adjacent)

### CODA — 101114765
- **Call**: Horizon Europe
- **Duration**: 2023-ongoing
- **Key Focus**: Adaptive digital assistant for air traffic controllers using neurophysiological assessment (workload, stress, fatigue, vigilance). Predicts future mental state.
- **Relevance**: Cross-domain transfer opportunity — CODA does for aviation what CORTEX does for SAR. Key conceptual reference for the Cognitive Twin approach. NOT in emergency/SAR domain.
- **CORDIS Link**: https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101114765
