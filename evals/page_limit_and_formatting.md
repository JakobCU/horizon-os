## Formatting Rules

### Font & Spacing
- **Font**: Times New Roman, Arial, or Calibri
- **Size**: Minimum 11pt for body text
- **Line spacing**: Single (or as specified in the template)
- **Margins**: At least 15mm on all sides (default template margins)
- **Headers/footers**: Use template-provided headers; do not modify

### Page Counting
- Counted from the first page of the section to the last
- Cover page, table of contents, and annexes are typically NOT counted
- Tables, figures, and references within the section ARE counted
- The reference list IS included in the page count (plan accordingly)

### Figures & Tables
- Must be legible at printed size (no "see enlarged version")
- Must have captions and be referenced in text
- Keep figures/tables compact — they consume valuable page space
- Prefer inline tables over full-page tables where possible

### References
- Use a consistent citation style (numbered or author-year)
- Keep reference list concise — only cite what you actually discuss
- Budget ~1 page for references within each major section

## Estimating Page Consumption

Rule of thumb for 11pt Times New Roman, single-spaced:
- ~550 words per page (pure text)
- A medium table consumes ~1/3 page
- A figure with caption consumes ~1/2 page
- A full WP description (objectives, tasks, deliverables, milestones table) consumes ~1.5-2 pages
